Why buy from DogDev

At Dog Dev, we don't make dog food. We make food for dogs. Our meals are made with high-quality, human-grade whole food ingridients that are more bioavailalble than fillers and other low-quality ingridients. We prepare and cook our meals in residential kitchens, tha same way human food is made, Each meal is filled hot to ensure safety and flash frozen to maintain freshness

How does the subscription plan work?

You tell us about your dog and we will create a meal plan that makes sense for both of you. We send the right amount of food to your door so you never have to worry about running out. Each meal is portioned perfectly so your pup always get the right amount of nutrients.

Can I request a sample to test?

We do not offer samples at this time, however, we do offer a 100% money back guarantee on your firts order if you and your dog are not completely satisfied with your experience. You can also start with a Top Plan, which is equivalent to 25% of a Premium Plan

How do I transition to DogDev

Start by mixing DogDev with with your dog's current food. DogDev should make up about 25% of the meal. Slowly increase this amount until your dog is eating 100% DogDev. If your dog has a sensitive stomach or has minor tummy issues, feed a smaller amount and increase slowly until he adapts to the new food

What it the difference the Top and Premium plans

If you want to feed your dog a 100% freshly prepared, complete and balanced diet, then our Premium Plan is ideal for you. If you want to add some freshly cooked food yo your pup's current diet, then our Top Plan is a great choice because it can be mixed in with their current food or served as an occasional meal for extra nutrition.-
